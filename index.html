Israeli Krav Maga Street Defense

Krav Maga is Hebrew for "Contact Combat". This system uses practical applications which can be easily acquired regardless of size and strength and teaches each student not only to protect themselves against an attacker but to help them physically and mentally prepare for the unexpected. This style of training does not require a uniform; we train in casual gym clothes and sneakers. We do our best to replicate real-life scenarios and environments. Our training comes directly from Israel and we teach Krav Maga in the fighting system's most modern evolution. Krav Maga has been tested and proven to be the most effective defense system on the planet. Classes are conducted in a safe and comfortable environment for all fitness and skill levels.

Krav Maga

In this class you will learn the fundamentals of the Krav Maga Street Defense system. All techniques will be taught to you in an easy-to-understand manner. Our simplified approach to training allows students to build confidence in skills they have developed in real-world applications. Krav Maga improves one’s overall strength, hand-eye coordination, and situational performance readiness. We practice realistic scenarios in a safe and fun environment.

Fighter Fit

We eliminate plateaus and push you forward faster than you thought possible. Our experienced instructors work with you every step of the way to help you achieve your goals and reach a whole new level of fitness.

Focus Mit

We are one of the only facilities to offer focus mitt training in a group class environment, which has proven to accelerate the development of striking fundamentals. Our instructors take you on an intense high-speed workout delivering combination strikes in sequences on moving targets which emulates striking a real opponent.

Weapons Defense

An all-level class dedicated only to defense against edged and impact weapons as well as hand guns and rifles. Instructors break down techniques step by step to give students a clear understanding on how to overcome a lethal attack.

About Us

Not only will we train you to be able to physically defend yourself against all manner of threats and assaults, we will also train you to have the same level of awareness as your IDF counterpart.
